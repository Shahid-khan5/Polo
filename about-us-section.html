Grace Santana

Led by the expertise of Grace G. Santana, we bring a wealth of experience and a commitment to delivering effective and impactful legal solutions across a diverse array of industries. Whether you are seeking guidance in commercial arbitration, civil litigation, administrative proceedings, corruption investigations, corporate matters, or human rights issues, our dedicated team is here to provide you with tailored counsel and unwavering support.

Grace G. Santana achieved her first law degree at Facultad Libre de Derecho de Monterrey in Monterrey, Mexico. She further pursued a master's law degree from the University of Alicante in Alicante, Spain, with an added dual degree from the University of Palermo in Palermo, Italy. Grace recently graduated from Columbia Law School's LL.M. program, where she proudly earned the Parker School Certificate of Recognition of Achievement in International and Comparative Law. During her studies, she participated in a study abroad program at New York University Law School, where she focused on the Regulation of Foreign Corrupt Practices Act "FCPA."

She is a seasoned legal professional with a proven track record in delivering results-driven solutions for complex legal matters. With over 8 years of experience, Grace's journey through the legal field has encompassed various industries, including construction, real estate, services, and more.

Fluency in English, Spanish, and Portuguese allows her to effectively communicate and collaborate across languages and cultures, a skill that proves invaluable in an increasingly interconnected world. We am here to provide expert guidance and tailored solutions that align with your unique needs.

Feel free to reach out to me for assistance, and let's embark on this legal journey together!
